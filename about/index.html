About Me

by Lynn

Hello!⌗

My name is Lynn Serizawa, and I am currently a Research Data/Operations Analyst at the Immune Tolerance Network (ITN) located in University of California, San Francisco (UCSF). I am currently seeking entry-level positions related to data science specifically in health (whether they be in academic research labs or companies building health devices).

This page will be split into two parts: my professional experience and what I am doing now.

Briefly, my chronological experience relevant to health data is as follows:

In high school, I was interested in neuroscience. Out of pure luck, my upstairs neighbor was Ben Barres, who noticed my interest in STEM subjects.

During the summer before college, I wrote a MATLAB script that measures axon-to-myelin ratios from user-generated measurements on ImageJ (or FIJI: Fiji Is Just ImageJ).

When I started college, my major was just philosophy.

Due to the pandemic (no not the Black Death), residence halls were closed and I flew to where my parents lived: Japan. There, I decided to take a gap year because the time-zone difference was making learning difficult. I would say that this is where my interest in data really kicked off. I used online resources to continue my learning. For example, I used Codecademy’s Data Science Career Path to gain more knowledge of data science. You can see some of my projects here.

Back at NYU, I tried to align my coursework to data. For example, I took Linear and Non-linear Optimization, where I learned how to apply minimization techniques to functions. This is relevant to data science because linear models work by reducing the total error between the model and data points. This creates an error function, where a minimization algorithm can be applied. I also took Probability and Statistics to learn the basics of, well, probability and statistics.

After graduation/now, I am working as a research data/operations analyst for the ITN at UCSF. I am open to new opportunities in data. Please don’t hesitate to reach out! See my contact info here.
